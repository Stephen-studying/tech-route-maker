# Demo Source Brief: Thesis Proposal Technical Route

This synthetic example shows how `tech-route-maker` can support a thesis proposal or research-plan route diagram.

## Research Topic

Design a technical route for a thesis proposal about data-driven degradation assessment of photovoltaic modules under outdoor operating conditions.

## Source Evidence

- Research question: how to link multi-source monitoring data with module degradation risk.
- Inputs: field inspection records, I-V curves, infrared images, weather logs, and maintenance notes.
- Preparation: data cleaning, temporal alignment, abnormal-sample screening, feature construction, and train/test split.
- Method: degradation indicators, feature selection, interpretable model, risk scoring, and uncertainty labeling.
- Validation: historical fault cases, cross-site comparison, ablation of feature groups, and expert review.
- Output: degradation-risk grade, key contributing factors, recommended inspection priority, and thesis-ready figure.
